Free Chlorine
  1. Rinse and fill the small comparator tube to 9mL mark with water to be tested.
  2. Add 5 drops of R-0001 and 5 drops of R-0002. Cap and invert to mix.
  3. Match color with color standard. Record free chlorine as parts per million (ppm)
Total Chlorine
  1. Using the same water, add 5 drops of R-0003. Cap and invert to mix. Match color and record as parts per million (ppm).

  1. The combined chlorine will be calculated for you.

pH
  1. Rinse and fill large comparator tube to 44 mL mark with water to be tested.
  2. Add 5 drops of R-0004. Cap and invert to mix.
  3. Compare to color standard and record pH units.

Alkalinity
  1. Rinse and fill large comparator tube to 25 mL mark with to be tested.
  2. Add 2 drops of R-0007. Swirl to mix.
  3. Add 5 drops of R-0008. The sample should turn green.
  4. Add R-0009 (keeping count of each drop). After each drop swirl. Do this until the sample turns red.
  5. Multiply drop count from step 4 by 10 and record this as ppm.

Stabalizer
  1. Fill tube to fill line (black mark) with sample water.
  2. Fill tube to second line with testing agent.
  3. Using the plunger, agitate for 30 seconds
  4. Push plunger to the bottom of the tube. Slowly lift the plunger until the black dot is visible.
  5. Record as ppm.

Calcuim Hardness
  1. Rinse and fill large comparator tube to 25 mL mark with water to be tested.
  2. Add 20 drops R-0010. Swirl to mix.
  3. Add 5 drops of R-0011L. Swirl to mix. If calcium hardness is present the sample will turn red.
  4. Add R-0012 dropwise (keep a count of each drop). After each drop swirl to mix. Continue until sample turns from red to blue.
  5. Multiply drop count from step 4 by 10 and record as ppm.
